AmbientPhile.com was born out of my love for recording and listening to ambient sounds. A couple of times a year, I travel to different countries, capturing the natural sounds of various environments without disturbing the setting or alerting those present. To try and capture every subtle nuance of these soundscapes, I use professional-grade microphones and recorders. I created this website to offer my recordings for sale and help fund my travels in making these ambient recordings.

Recordings
Each recording is a single continuous session, often exceeding 10 hours, maintaining the natural atmosphere without artificial breaks or edits. These recordings are in 32-bit float WAV format at a sample rate of 96,000 Hz, ensuring high-fidelity representation that preserves the authenticity and tranquility of the soundscapes. Each download includes the original WAV files and a joint, tagged FLAC file edited to eliminate any clipping.
