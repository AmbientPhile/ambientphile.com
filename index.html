AmbientPhile.com was born out of my love for recording and listening to ambient sounds. I travel the world, capturing the pure, natural sounds of various environments with professional-grade microphones and recorders to preserve every subtle nuance without disturbing the setting or alerting those present. I created this website to offer my recordings for sale, sharing the beauty of these soundscapes with others.

Each recording is a single continuous session, often exceeding 10 hours, maintaining the natural atmosphere without artificial breaks or edits. These recordings are in 32-bit float WAV format at a sample rate of 96,000 Hz, ensuring high-fidelity representation that preserves the authenticity and tranquility of the soundscapes. Each download includes the original WAV files and a joint, tagged FLAC file edited to eliminate any clipping.

Recordings available for purchase are listed below with sample previews and details such as title, duration, size, and price (commercial price is three times the personal price). For privacy reasons, the exact location and recording date are left out. Each sample includes short segments from the actual recording.
