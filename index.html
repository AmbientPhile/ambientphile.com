My love for listening to ambient sounds inspired the creation of AmbientPhile.com. I travel the world to capture the pure, natural sounds of different environments, recording unique soundscapes without disturbing the setting or alerting those present. Below, you can learn more about the equipment I use, my recordings, and how to buy and download them. At the bottom of the page, you'll also find a sample of each recording available for sale, along with some information about each one.
Equipment
Utilizing high-quality, professional-grade microphones and recorders, I ensure that every subtle nuance of the environment is preserved. This commitment to superior equipment guarantees that my recordings capture the true essence of each location.
Recordings
Each recording is captured as a single, continuous session that often exceeds 10 hours, which maintains the natural atmosphere without introducing artificial breaks or edits. These recordings are made in 32-bit float WAV format at a sample rate of 96,000 Hz, ensuring a high-fidelity representation that preserves the authenticity and tranquility of the natural soundscapes. Additionally, each download includes a joint and tagged FLAC file, which has been edited to eliminate any clipping, alongside the original WAV files.
