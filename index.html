About
Welcome! I specialize in capturing the pure, unaltered sounds of various environments across the globe. My passion for natural ambience drives me to travel extensively, recording the unique soundscapes of each location without disturbing the natural setting or alerting those present.
Contact
Equipment
Utilizing high-quality, professional-grade microphones and recorders, I ensure that every subtle nuance of the environment is preserved. This commitment to superior equipment guarantees that my recordings capture the true essence of each location.
Recording Sessions
Each recording is one continuous session, often extending beyond 10 hours in length. This approach allows for an uninterrupted, authentic auditory experience, reflecting the natural atmosphere without any artificial breaks or edits.
Audio Format
My recordings are meticulously captured in 32-bit float WAV format to provide the highest possible quality. This ensures an immersive and detailed auditory experience, maintaining the authenticity and tranquility of the natural soundscapes.
