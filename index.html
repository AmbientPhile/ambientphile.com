Equipment
Utilizing high-quality, professional-grade microphones and recorders, I ensure that every subtle nuance of the environment is preserved. This commitment to superior equipment guarantees that my recordings capture the true essence of each location.
Recordings
Each recording is one continuous session, often exceeding 10 hours, preserving the natural atmosphere without artificial breaks or edits. Recordings are captured in 32-bit float WAV format at a 96000 Hz sample rate to ensure high quality, maintaining the authenticity and tranquility of the natural soundscapes.
